## Search Strategy Tips

1. **Specific → Broad** — Exact match first, then widen scope
2. **Compound queries** — "react deploy aws" beats "deploy" alone
3. **Noun + Verb forms** — Try both "deployment" and "deploy"
4. **Upstream + Downstream** — What comes before and after this task?
5. **English is denser** — Non-English requests should also search in English
6. **Vary word order** — "react deploy" and "deploy react" can return different results
